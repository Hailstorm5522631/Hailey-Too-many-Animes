Twisted wonderland

Twisted Wonderland is an game, manga, and anime that consists of characters in collage based off of Disney villians. There are seven houses/dorms based off of a Disney Villain.

The house known as Heartslabyul was founded by the Queen of Hearts from Alice in Wonderland. This house consists of five main characters, Riddle Rosehearts, Ace Troppla, Deuce Spade, Trey Clover, and Cater Diamond. Riddle Rosehearts is a second-year Housewarden, which is the equivilant to the head of the dorm. Trey Clover is the third-year Vice Housewarden. Cater Diamond is a third-Year, and Deuce Spade and Ace Troppla are first-years. There are 810 rules in the Heartslabyul dorm, and an event that happens called "The Unbirthday Party"

The Next house is called Savannaclaw, which was Founded by the King of Beasts, Scar, From the Lion King. This house consists of three main characters, Leona Kingscholar, Ruggie Bucchi, and Jack Howl. Leona Kingscholar is the third-year Housewarden who is a Lion beastman. The term "Beastman" is used to identify those who are part animal, part Human. Ruggie Bucchi is the second-year Heyna Beastman. Jack Howl is a first-year wolf Beastman.
