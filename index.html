Twisted Wonderland

Twisted Wonderland is an game, manga, and anime that consists of characters in collage based off of Disney villians. There are seven houses/dorms based off of a Disney Villain. Each character has a uniqu magic that is known as a signature spell. It is developed by the user's interest and power. The Hall of mirrors is the only place that is actually on campus. Inside the Hall of Mirrors, there are mirrors that act as a portal to the other dorms, each house with their own mirror.

The house known as Heartslabyul was founded by the Queen of Hearts from "Alice in Wonderland". This house consists of five main characters, Riddle Rosehearts, Ace Troppla, Deuce Spade, Trey Clover, and Cater Diamond. Riddle Rosehearts is a second-year Housewarden, which is the equivilant to the head of the dorm. Trey Clover is the third-year Vice Housewarden. Cater Diamond is a third-Year, and Deuce Spade and Ace Troppla are first-years. Riddle's signature spell is called "Off With Your Head", which puts a collar around the person or people who gets hit by it's neck and prevents the victim from using any magic. Trey's signature spell is called "Pain the Roses", which allows him to change the properties, like taste, texture, color, and shape, of an item for a limited amount of time. Cater's signature spell is called "Split Card". If he uses it, he can create clones of himself that can act independantly. Deuce's signature spell is called "Double Down". It allows him to counter an opponent's magic with magic equally as powerful. The stronger the opponent, the more power this spell gets. Ace's signature spell is "Joker Snatch", which allows him to temporarily use another person's signature spell. There are 810 rules in the Heartslabyul dorm, and an event that happens called "The Unbirthday Party", which happens when it is a day no one has a birthday. The Housewarden is very strict on the 810 rules, if you break one, it will be off with your head!

The Next house is called Savannaclaw, which was Founded by the King of Beasts, Scar, From the "Lion King". This house consists of three main characters, Leona Kingscholar, Ruggie Bucchi, and Jack Howl. Leona Kingscholar is the third-year Housewarden who is a Lion beastman. The term "Beastman" is used to identify those who are part animal, part Human. Ruggie Bucchi is the second-year Heyna Beastman. Jack Howl is a first-year wolf Beastman. Leona's signature spell is called "The King's Roar". This makes whatever he touches turn to sand and dust. Ruggie's signature spell is called "Laugh with Me!", which makes others mimic his physical motions. Jack's signature spell is called "Unleash the Beast", which allows him to transform into a large white wolf. This world's equivilant to Soccer/Football is spelldrive. Spelldrive is a sport with six people on each side. You have to use magic to score a goal in the other's ring. Savannaclaw has students that excell in sporting events like Spelldrive and Track & Field.

The third house is named Octavinelle. Octavinelle was founded by the Sea Witch, Ursula, From "The Little Mermaid". This house also has three main students, all second-years, Azul Ashengrotto, Jade Leech, and Floyd Leech. The Housewarden is Azul Ashengrotto, who is an octopus-merman. Jade Leech is the Vice Housewarden, who is an eel-merman. Floyd Leech is Jade's twin brother and also an eel-merman. Azul's signature spell is called 'It's a Deal!" which disables an ability or talent from anyone who signs a contract with him. Jade's signature spell is called "Shock the Heart", which forces the target to speak the truth if they look into his left eye. Floyd's signature spell is called "Bind the Heart", which interferes with the target's magic, causing the attack to miss. The Ocavinelle dormitory is very unique because it is the only dormatory that is fully underwater, which allows the students to turn into their mermaid/merman form when they are inside their dorm. The students of Octavinelle run a student buessness called "The Mostro Lounge", which is basically a small cafe on in the dorm.
